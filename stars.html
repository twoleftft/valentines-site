"When the Stars Aligned for Us"

That night, the sky was quieter than usual,
Four planets wove our fate in threads of light...
Mercury lingered in the air,
unspoken, like the years between us—
a quiet pull, waiting to be answered.
Venus shimmered in your eyes,
a quiet glow, soft with regret.
We laughed, just us, in a near-empty room...
Mars burned somewhere between us,
each mile down Fijai, a silent battle—
not against fear, but against waiting any longer.
And when the words finally came out,
Unplanned...Unshaped...but completely certain,
the stars simply watched—
as if they already knew.
